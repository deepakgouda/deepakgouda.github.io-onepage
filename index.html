I am a quantitative strategist working at the algorithmic trading firm Kivi Capital. I am broadly interested in probabilistic sampling techniques and event simulations.

I primarily work on statistical analysis and modelling of financial time series data in Forex and Commodity segments. I have experience across a range of domains crucial to a trade's life cycle, starting from data capture over high speed networks to formulation and execution of a carefully crafted trade signal.

I did my Bachelor thesis under the guidance of Dr. Kalpesh Kapoor where we developed a lightweight, modular framework to generate network-level synthetic data of any Blockchain topology. We further proposed a novel validation method for any Blockchain framework.

I graduated with a Bachelor in Technology degree, majoring in Mathematics and Computing from Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati, India in 2020. Feel free to check my CV or drop me a Hi! at deepakgouda1729@gmail.com.

I like exploring things and publish my findings and summaries at Nightly Blogs. I travel occasionally and nurture photography as a hobby. Feel free to browse my work at the Gallery.
